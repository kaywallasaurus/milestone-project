about me


I love producing clean and creative code. I started learning how to code with Skillcrush and even taught myself a few skills after that. I love what I do, now more than ever and I’m looking forward to what the future may hold for me in the world of development.

I have two cats named Binx and Mouse and they can often be found sitting by my side while I code. I embarked on my coding journey in 2018 and I’ve loved every minute of it. I’m hoping to build a freelance design and development business where I can help people build the websites of their dreams.


I look forward to working with you!
